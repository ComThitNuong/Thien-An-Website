About Us

Welcome to Bo 7 Mon or in English, 7 courses of beef, where culinary artistry meets Vietnamese tradition. Our journey is a tapestry woven with resilience, cultural heritage, and a love for authentic cuisine.

Founded by a Vietnamese refugee, Bo 7 Mon emerged as a beacon of culinary excellence in Orange County, California. Our passion for the art of "7 Courses of Beef" became the cornerstone of our establishment.

Our commitment to authenticity and a fusion of traditional recipes with a modern twist has transformed Bo 7 Mon into a culinary empire with multiple locations across Orange County.

Help us continue our journey where every dish tells a story of heritage and every meal is a celebration of the remarkable journey that brought us to your table.
