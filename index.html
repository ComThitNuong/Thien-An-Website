Menu

World Famous catfish: Baked catfish charbroiled to perfection.


Specialty: Bo 7 Mon

Dish 1: Gói Tôm Thiên Ân - a lovely combination of vinegary and citrus flavors from the dressing, minty tones from the chopped up herbs, nuttiness and crunch from the peanuts, meatiness from the shrimp, and sweetness from the carrots, radish, and celery.


Dish 2: Bò nhùng dâm; Beef fondue; cook these shabu shabu style with a boiling broth. The beef is thin so it cooks quickly.


Dish 3: Bò nớng vĩ; Beef marinated with lemon grass; grill these on a sizzling hot grill bbq style.


Dish 4: Bò nuớng lá lốt; Beef cooked in la lot leaves. The la lot leaves, which are like grape leaves, keep the beef moist and gives it a nice herbal flavor to compliment the meat flavor.


Dish 5: Bò nớng mỡ chài; Ground beef meatballs that have a great garlicky, caramelized profile.


Dish 6: Bò chả đùm; Beef pate mixed with nuts, mushrooms, and spices. Not bad though I can see that the texture can be off setting for some.


Dish 7: Cháo bò; A warm, comforting, homey beef soup with alphabet noodles.
